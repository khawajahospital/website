About Khawaja Hospital

Khawaja Hospital is a community-focused healthcare centre offering a wide range of medical services including emergency care, diagnostics, surgery, maternity, and outpatient services. Our team is composed of experienced physicians, nurses, and allied health professionals committed to evidence-based, compassionate care.
